CMPM 170: Game Design Studio I project
Full walkthrough
Hex Harmonia is a 3D puzzle games that player needs to resolve all the puzzles in the hexagon to escape from the room. There are six unique puzzle rooms waiting for you.

My contribution

  • Worked with a team of 9 developers in CMPM 170 to create a 3D puzzle game.
  • Used Unity, C#, and GitHub throughout the development.
  • Developed scripted game events of two puzzle rooms and 3D player controller.
  • Designed and implemented one puzzle room (raccoon rhythm puzzle room).
  • Coordianted with sound artists during developing to make sure we were at the same page.
