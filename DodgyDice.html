Dodgy Dice (2022)

Play Now
GMTK 2022 GAME JAM projects

Dodgy Dice is a top down, vampire-survivor-like bullet hell where the character is a multi sided die. Each time a die is rolled, a number is generated and used to fuel various behaviors of the game.

My contribution

  • Developed with Godot and GDscript.
  • Brainstormed, desigend and finished the whole game within 48 hours with another 10 group memebers.
  • I was responsible for dice randomnizing, dice spawning, and UI/Menu code.
  • I worked closely as programmers iterating through gameplay features and build mechanics.
