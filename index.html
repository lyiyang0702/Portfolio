Dodgy Dice


Dodgy Dice is a top down, vampire-survivor-like bullet hell where the character is a multi sided die. Each time a die is rolled, a number is generated and used to fuel various behaviors of the game.
Where is My Body


Where is my body is a 2D Endless runner game that the character is a ghost trying to find its body. The ghost has to avoid the obstacles on its way by jumping.
